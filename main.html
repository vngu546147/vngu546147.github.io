Our Services

We always do the best we can to make sure people leave our food luck satisfied and with their tummies full. People think we are a word that we hear everyday: Original. People admire us because we always do something differently than the other restaurants or grocery stores.

GLOBE is a mobile eatery located in Downtown Phoenix, however, we are also available to book for parties and other events. Contact Us by submitting a form either to set a date for catering or just for more information. We offer our own menus or can instead design a custom menu for the special event. Pricing will be determined after discussion on details.
